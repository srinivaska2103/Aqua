Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our aquatic services.

How often should my aquarium be cleaned?

Most aquariums benefit from professional maintenance on a weekly or monthly basis, depending on the size of the tank, filtration system, and livestock population. We provide a customized schedule after our initial assessment.

Do you provide fish and plants?

Yes, we are a full-service provider. We offer a wide range of healthy livestock, including fish, aquatic plants, and corals, as well as high-quality accessories to ensure your ecosystem is complete.

Do you service commercial aquariums?

Absolutely. We have extensive experience maintaining aquariums in professional environments, including corporate offices, luxury hotels, restaurants, and public spaces, ensuring they always look their absolute best.
